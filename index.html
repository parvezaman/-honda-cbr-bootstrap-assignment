Honda CBR 300R

A single is the perfect choice for a bike like the CBR300R. It's light, simple, fuel efficient and features a broad powerband with plenty of low-revving torque—just what you want in your first motorcycle, but geared for comfortable cruising at highway speeds.

What do you need to bring to purchase a motorcycle
  • Make sure you bring two forms of valid I.D.
  • Bring the money you can put down on your new motorcycle (this can be cash, cashier’s check, credit card, personal check etc…)
  • If you'd like to take the motorcycle out on a test ride, it would be wise to bring your riding gear (helmet, jacket, boots, gloves, etc.).
  • If you are planning on financing your new ride you can save yourself some time by getting pre-approved through our online credit application (just in case the bank needs more information, such as proof of income).
  • We recommend scheduling one-on-one time with your Product Specialist to ensure they are available to help you when you come in. If you haven’t been in before, contact us to schedule your one-on-one time.
  • Bring your sense of fun!
